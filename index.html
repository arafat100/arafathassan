GIS Portfolio

Westerville
Transparency Hub

This is a central hub created by ArcGIS Hub where the community of Westerville, OH can visit and find out key items such as Public Safety, Utlities, Service, Planning & Development, etc around the city. I maintain and update all the GIS data which the public views. In addition, I am monitoring (and modifying if needed) the scripts that run for each departments at a database level (SQL Server). The GIS SQL Server is extracting data from various sources and transforming it in a way that is required for the public to see such the public safety data, service department data, etc. Later, I am able to display the data in a fun and an interactive way for the public to consume via ArcGIS web applications.

County Parcel
Data Import

The City of Westerville gathers parcel data from both Franklin and Delaware County's auditors website since the city shares both counties geographically. I am using ETL method to capture this data. Both counties have their parcel data in their GIS which contains the REST Web Services. I found a way to gather the parcel data using the REST Web Services provided by the counties within ArcGIS Pro Modelbuilder. Once the data is captured, I am using a view to merge the data from both counties and transforming it into one single layer used by the City of Westerville.

Custom Printing
Widget

The City of Westerville GIS is migrating to WebGIS due to it's major benefits for its internal users such as consuming map services from anywhere and anytime. However, the internal users still needed to print out paper maps for time to time. Therefore, I created a custom printing widget within the city's WebGIS (ArcGIS Enterprise). With the custom printing tool, I was able to provide the end users differnt layout options such as adding the city's logo, picking different page sizes, etc.

Custom Imagery
Widget

The City of Westerville uses/needs aerial imagery in order to better view the city from a bird's-eye view. The city also uses oblique version of the aerial imagery to better understand what is on the ground. This type of imagery can assist departments such as Fire or Police to better understand a serious situation and make a quick decision that can result in life or death situation. I have implemented an ArcGIS Web AppBuilder custom widget provided by our aerial imagery provider, Nearmap, to be able to view the oblique imagery for departments within the city.

Cityworks
Map

The City of Westerville uses a computerized maintenance management system (CMMS) called Cityworks for asset management and permitting.
